# Portfolio — Tosin Adedokun

## Design Context

### Users
Startup founders, hiring managers, and engineers at AI/early-stage companies scanning portfolios on desktop and mobile. They are skeptical, busy, and have seen a thousand "AI-engineer" templates this week. The site has 30 seconds to prove that the person behind it builds with taste, ships fast, and is worth a reply.

### Brand Personality
Loud. Confident. A 19-year-old solopreneur with receipts. Editorial swagger over corporate polish — closer to a print zine or a trader terminal than a SaaS landing page.

Three words: **loud, opinionated, built-from-scratch.**

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Dark. Warm near-black, not blue-black, not pure #000. Think newsprint ink on coated stock at midnight.
- **Direction:** Brutalist tech-zine. Massive condensed display headlines, monospace meta in the margins, hard hairline rules, ledger-style data tables, marquee bands, numbered sections like a magazine index.
- **Loudness comes from scale and contrast**, not from glow effects or gradients. The headline is the explosion, not the background.
- **Anti-references (do NOT do):**
  - Glassmorphism, blurred orbs, cursor-following glow
  - Gradient text (`background-clip: text`)
  - Side-stripe borders on cards
  - Identical rounded card grids with icon + heading + text
  - Monospace-everywhere "developer aesthetic"
  - Cyan-on-dark, purple-to-blue gradients, neon accents

### Design Principles
1. **Type does the heavy lifting.** Headlines bleed off the grid. Body type is generous and readable.
2. **Hairlines, not boxes.** Use 1px rules and whitespace to separate, not container cards.
3. **Receipts everywhere.** Numbers, dates, project counts, locations — show the data, monospaced, like a dashboard.
4. **One accent, used sparingly.** Hi-vis yellow (`oklch(0.88 0.18 95)`) for the 10% that must be remembered. Everything else is cream on warm black.
5. **Motion is editorial, not decorative.** Reveal-on-scroll, marquees, hover state changes. No bouncing, no parallax orbs.
